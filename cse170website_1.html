~ Monaco ~

The scenic beauty, the F1 track and the riches, it was simply mesmerizing. I wish I could watch the Monaco Grand Prix there, would've been a once in a lifetime experience.

Travel Tips

  1. Monaco is known for its hilly terrain, so wear comfortable shoes for walking. I got mine from ON.
  2. Respect the Royal Family and avoid making jokes about the royal family, and if you encounter them, use the proper respectful greeting.
  3. Always carry a copy of your photo ID or your passport for security purposes.
